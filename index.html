SaneBox Setup Guide

Follow these simple steps to set up SaneBox and organize your inbox efficiently. It takes just a few minutes!

1. Sign Up for SaneBox

  • Visit www.sanebox.com and click "Start Free Trial" to begin your 14-day free trial.
  • Enter your email address (works with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Apple Mail, or any IMAP/Exchange server).

2. Connect Your Email

  • Log in with your email provider (e.g., Gmail OAuth) or enter your email password securely. SaneBox uses encryption to protect your data.
  • Grant permissions for SaneBox to access your email via IMAP for sorting. It only analyzes email headers, not content.

3. Let SaneBox Analyze Your Email

SaneBox will review your email history to identify important emails. This takes a few minutes and happens automatically.

4. Check New Folders

  • Look for folders like “SaneLater” (for less urgent emails) in your email client. Sync/Refresh your folders if they don’t show up.
  • Drag any misplaced emails to the right folder (e.g., Inbox) to train SaneBox.

5. Customize Settings

  • Log into the SaneBox Dashboard to:
    • Enable folders (e.g., “SaneNews” for newsletters, “SaneBlackHole” for spam).
    • Set reminders (e.g., for unanswered emails).
    • Configure “Do Not Disturb” or cloud storage for attachments.
  • Adjust settings to match your workflow.

6. Review the Daily Digest

Check the SaneBox Digest (emailed daily) to see emails moved to SaneLater. Move any emails to the correct folders to improve sorting.

7. Explore Key Features

  • SaneBlackHole: Drag unwanted emails here to block future messages from that sender.
  • SaneReminders: BCC addresses like “2days.resend@sanebox.com” for follow-up reminders.
  • Snooze Emails: Use folders like “SaneTomorrow” to defer emails.
  • Deep Clean: Delete old emails to free up space.

8. Test During the Free Trial

  • Use the 14-day trial to test and refine SaneBox. Train it by moving emails to the right folders.
  • Contact support@sanebox.com for help if needed.

9. Choose a Plan

After the trial, pick a plan (e.g., Snack, Lunch, or Dinner) based on your needs.

Tips:
  • Train SaneBox regularly by moving emails to the correct folders.
  • Use the dashboard to tailor features to your needs.
  • If you cancel during the trial, your email setup reverts to its original state.

Enjoy a cleaner inbox with SaneBox!

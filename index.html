The Tesla Robotaxi: Austin’s Newest Star

Posted on June 23, 2025

Picture this: you’re in Austin, Texas, and a sleek, driverless Tesla Model Y pulls up to whisk you away. No driver, no fuss—just you and the future. That’s the Tesla Robotaxi, which officially hit the streets on June 22, 2025, and it’s already turning heads.

Tesla’s Robotaxi isn’t just a car; it’s a glimpse into tomorrow. Powered by Full Self-Driving (FSD) tech, it uses cameras and AI to navigate Austin’s bustling streets with ease. Forget clunky lidar or radar—this baby sees the world like we do, and it’s smarter than ever. Launched as part of Tesla’s ambitious autonomous ride-hailing network, it’s currently cruising in select Austin neighborhoods, with remote operators keeping an eye on things for extra safety.

Why’s everyone buzzing? For starters, it’s eco-friendly, running on Tesla’s electric Model Y platform. Plus, it’s affordable—rides cost a flat $4.20 (nice touch, Elon). And let’s talk about the vibe: sliding into a Robotaxi feels like stepping into a sci-fi flick. The interior is sleek, the ride is smooth, and you can kick back while Tesla’s AI handles the wheel.

Looking ahead, Tesla’s got big plans. The Cybercab, a dedicated Robotaxi model, is slated for production in 2026 and will cost under $30,000 to buy. For now, Austinites can book rides through the Tesla app, with expansion to other cities on the horizon. Whether you’re a tech geek or just hate parking, the Robotaxi is here to change the game.

Ready to ride? Scroll down to learn how to book your first trip!

What Makes Robotaxi Epic?

Smart AI Vision

How to Ride the Robotaxi

Pricing

Rides are a flat $4.20 in Austin (as of June 23, 2025). The upcoming Cybercab will retail for under $30,000 (production starts 2026).

Booking Steps

  1. Grab the Tesla app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Log in or create a Tesla account.
  3. Tap “Robotaxi” and pick your Austin pickup spot.
  4. Confirm and enjoy your driverless ride!

Note: Currently available in select Austin areas. More cities coming soon!
